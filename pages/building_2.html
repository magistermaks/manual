Shield
20m

A shield is used to protect yourself from bombings coming from abroad, it can shoot down bombs both normal and dirty, but is powerless against incoming nuclear warheads. When a bomb enters the protected area and the owner of the shield wants to shoot it down one bomb will be used up and the incoming bomb will be destroyed.

Shield protects an area in Manhattan distance of 5 tiles from itself (marked on the image with a gray line). Be aware that because of the way bombs work even though the \B\ is inside the protected area it can still be destroyed by shooting the A point. To make sure your buildings are safe place them at a distance of 4 tiles from the shield, like the two factories.


Parliament
10m

Allows you to buy upgrades, those upgrades will works as long as you have at least one parliament in your country. See the list of available upgrades in the upgrades chapter.


City
100m

You can summon a new unit every turn from it in the same way you do it from the capital. Also similarly to capitals it cannot be destroyed by shooting with ammunition nor bombing, only a nuclear strike can destroy it. It cannot be demolished once build. When occupied it grants control over some of the terrain surrounding it. Cities can be used to connect a colony with the rest of the country, learn more in the cities chapter.


Fortress
50m

Works similarly to cities but doesn't allow for new units to be summoned. You can upgrade it to a city later by paying an additional 60m. Unlike cities, you cannot move your capital there, learn more in the cities chapter.


Pier
5m

A 1x1 rotatable water structure that allows you to dock your ships and link colonies, learn more in the water chapter.
