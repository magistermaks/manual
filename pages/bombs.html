BOMBS

deadly fireworks from heaven

This chapter depicts how bomb explosions work, to learn how to send those bombs to their target see the Rocket Launcher (\N\) in the building chapters.

The tiles marked black inside of the pattern on those drawings are covered with radiation after detonation, all other ones are just destroyed. When even a single tile of a building is destroyed the whole building is as well.

normal bomb

Destroys a relatively small area and creates no radiation.

dirty bomb

Destroys the same area as a normal bomb but completely covers is with radiation. Can be used to destroy material mines of the enemy for good.

nuclear warhead

Destroys an enormous area and covers a large part of it with radiation, a direct strike of this fat boy can destroy entire cities and the capital in nuclear flames. Learn more in the cities chapter.
