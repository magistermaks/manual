COLONIES

the sun shall never set over your empire

Colony is a territory not directly connected with the rest of the country but with, for example, a bridge over a river.

Your colony needs to always stay connected. You can use bridges (as on the image), piers (see water) or place a city/fortress inside (see cities). The cities and/or fortresses are the safest way to ensure that your colony never gets disconnected.


disconnected colonies

When a colony gets disconnected for all intents and purposes it should be treated as no man's land. You can't use the buildings located there, build new ones or demolish old ones, get materials from local mines, your units move as if outside of your country, you can't equip armor, it can be colonized with normal colonization, etc. But once you regain connection with the lost territory everything gets back to normal. Note that as a disconnected colony may be treated a part of your normal territory that was separated from you, for example, by exchanging it or being cut off with military colonization.

Borders don't connect diagonally!
