UPGRADES

list of parliament upgrades

For you to buy and use those upgrades you need at last one parliament in your country (learn more in building), if you loose it all your upgrades deactivate but you don't loose them, once you rebuild the parliament they will start working again. When you buy an upgrade put your symbol next to the upgrade's on the parliament page/section of the map.

Double Colonization
20m

Allows you to colonize two times per turn independently of the colonization type.

Recycling
10m

Allows you to get back 60% of the price when demolishing a building, and get 3m instead of 2m when collecting Surface Deposits (\SURFACE_DEPOSIT\).

Expanded Storage
5m

Allows you to store up to 20m in the local warehouse.

Expanded \N\ Range
15m

Extends the range of Rocket Launchers (\N\) by 5 tiles.

Expanded \S\ Range
10m

Extends the range of Shields (\S\) by 1 tile.

Longer Bridges
10m

Allows you to construct bridges up to 10 tiles long.

Safe Refining
5m

With this upgrade your Bomb Factories (\B\) don't explode when destroyed.

Tactical Maps
10m

All your units gain an extra movement point, learn more in units chapter.
