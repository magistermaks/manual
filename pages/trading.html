TRADING

and gifts

Trading in this game is allowed, even encouraged, but the whole system is based only on honor, there is no requirement for anyone to go through on their agreements and promises. You can send some resources to another player, but they can refuse to pay you for it.

You can only send resources to countries with which you have direct contact with (the borders touch horizontally or vertically, or are connected by a bridge). If the only way between you and your trading partner is through another player you will need to strike an agreement with them to be your middleman.

To send something (a, z, b, n, u, r, or m) to another player you have to denote it using the trade arrow in your player statistics, see it in the statistics chapter. Be very precise here, the game master has the right and duty to cancel any transfer that is in any way incorrect. You also cannot modify it after your turn ends.

At the end of the round, the game master executes all the valid trade arrows by moving the resources and erasing the arrows.

If the transfer is impossible to carry out either because the connection between the two countries has been lost by the end of the round or because origin no longer has all the promised resources the arrow is cancelled and erased, even if its cyclical.

You can have multiple resources on one arrow ().
